Inspired by Joel Runyon to create an impossible list of goals and things I want to *experience* in life.


What is impossible list?

It is a list of things I've been putting off because I might have told myself it is impossible and I couldn't do them.


Impossible list is different from bucket list because it evolves as you add more items in, as you incorporate them into your life. Bucket list on the other hand is static and just a list of things you want to check off.


Rules:

  1. Cross off items achieved
  2. Add new items as I keep exploring the universe
  3. No time pressure
  4. Enjoy the process as much as I can
